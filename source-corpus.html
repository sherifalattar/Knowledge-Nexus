00 Licensing & attribution

The one boundary that governs the whole corpus

IR-DRG is licensed, proprietary, and not reproduced

The IR-DRG grouper — Solventum (formerly 3M Health Information Systems) International Refined Diagnosis Related Groups — is licensed intellectual property. The algorithm, diagnosis tables, MDC assignments, CC/MCC logic, PCAT tables, severity splits, and DRG weights are not reproduced in any page. No legal open-source IR-DRG grouper exists.

The diagnosis → MDC → base-group architecture shown in the maps is a navigational and educational layer built on public ICD-10-CM (CDC/NCHS) and CMS MS-DRG Appendix B mappings. It is DRG-inspired; it is not the licensed IR-DRG table.

“This model is DRG-inspired but not IR-DRG. Every operational diagnosis-to-MDC and base-group assignment must be verified against the licensed IR-DRG DX table or grouper.” — “IR-DRG” and the Solventum name are trademarks of Solventum, used here for reference and attribution only.
